⛳ How This Pool Works

Picking Players

Each participant picks one player from each of 6 tiers. Tiers are set by the admin based on betting odds — Tier 1 is the top 10 favourites, Tier 6 the biggest longshots.

Scoring

Pool score = combined total-to-par of your best 4 of 6 picks. The two worst scores are dropped. Lower is better. Picks shown in gold are your 4 counting scores.

Elimination

If 3 or more picks miss the cut after Round 2, you are eliminated for that tournament.

Tiebreaker

If two participants tie, whoever guessed closest to the actual winning score wins.

Wild Card

The last slot in Tier 6 is a Wild Card — the participant can pick any player in the field.

✏️ Manual Score Entry

Enter scores from the PGA Tour app. Use E for even, -8 for 8 under, +3 for 3 over. Toggle CUT for missed cuts.

Hit Refresh to load the tournament leaderboard

Pool Setup

Select the tournament, enter each participant's name and tiebreaker guess, and add their 6 picks. Lowest 4 of 6 count. 3+ cuts = eliminated. Ties broken by closest winning score guess.

✏️ Pool Name

The name shown at the top of the leaderboard for all participants.

🏌️ Tournament

Choose the tournament — tells ESPN which event to pull scores from.

📊 Player Tiers

Enter the 6 tiers for this tournament. Add player names and optional odds. Once saved, participant picks will use dropdowns from these lists. Hit Copy Tier List to paste into your group chat.

🔗 Share Leaderboard

Generate a link with all picks pre-loaded. Anyone opens it on their phone and hits Refresh — no account needed. If over 4,000 characters, shorten at bit.ly before sharing.

Click 'Generate Link' to create a shareable URL
